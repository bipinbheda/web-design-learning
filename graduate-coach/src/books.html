    The knowledge and wisdom we’ve gleaned over the past seven years have now been encapsulated into two books.

    The Student Book and The Graduate Book, written by the founder of Graduate Coach, Chris Davies, are available now. The Student Book shows students how they can spend their time at university laying the foundations needed for successful job seeking after graduation. It covers the nine key skills every employer looks for in a new hire, then tells you exactly how to develop these skills and sell them on your CV and in an interview. The Student Book has made it on to the Fupping.com list of the 21 books every 20-year-old should read! Both The Student Book and The Graduate Book have made it on to the Boove.co.uk list of career books to help you find your calling!

    The Graduate Book guides graduates on the steps they should take, and the techniques they should apply, to make a success of their first job. Through 13 key lessons learnt along the way, Chris shares his own truly inspiring career journey, including the ups and downs, to help you get to your ‘where you want to be’ quickly and with less trauma.

    Author Chris Davies

    Head Coach & Founder at Graduate Coach

    Chris Davies has been helping students and graduates with their interview preparation for the past 10 years and has successfully helped 450+ candidates to get their dream graduate job.

    Before becoming an interview coach, Chris spent 30 years in advertising and publishing as a trusted advisor to companies including Unilever, Barclays, Tescos and many other Times Top 100 companies.

    During this time, Chris conducted hundreds of job interviews and hired 300+ people and he trained people on how to successfully pitch to clients and win new business.

    Chris draws upon these experiences to offer his highly recommended graduate interview coaching service.
