    • Digital Internship

    Our Digital Internship programme is offered in our London office or remotely online and covers ​​all the essential digital skills you need to become highly employable in today's market.

    What you will gain:

    • Industry-recognised digital certifications.
    • Experience gained through practical assignments.
    • A transformed CV, comprehensive career coaching.

    Programme Overview

    Unlike every other digital skills training course, this programme will help you to beat the ‘experience paradox’ that makes it difficult for those new to the industry to land their first job. Our solution helps you to overcome the hurdle of not being able to get experience (due to not having experience) by providing you with an assessed digital internship giving you the opportunity to work on real-life projects to enrich your CV with the skills and experience employers need. As well as gaining certifications, experience and skills you will also receive career coaching by the UK’s leading graduate coaching company. By the end of the programme, you will be interview confident and ready to strategically navigate your job search. Flexible finance options are available.

    What’s covered?
    • Fact-finding to discuss your career preferences.
    • Emotional Intelligence assessments to uncover your personal drivers.
    • Skills audit assessment to uncover your work-related skills and experience.
    • Self-belief and confidence development, which continues throughout your coaching.

    Who can apply

    This is an intensive digital marketing programme for those who are serious about developing their skills and boosting their employability. This programme is for:

    • Students or graduates who are interested in pursuing a career in digital marketing
    • Career changers who need industry experience
    • Anyone who wants to upskill in digital and apply their skills to their current digital or non-digital role

    Simple admissions process

    The application process for this programme is easy. First, you’ll have a brief call with our head coach Chris Davies, who will tell you more about Graduate Coach and discuss your career aspirations and suitability for this programme. Then you’ll be enrolled in the programme, be given access to our learning resources and be introduced to your digital marketing coach.
