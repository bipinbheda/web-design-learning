    • Workshop

    Unsure of what you want to do? This workshop will help get you on your way.

    Most graduates (90%) are unsure of what to do next. This career guidance workshop uses a practical and personalised approach to help you put together a plan for discovering your ideal career.

    What you will learn

    Hosted in Central London or remote online: One day with lifelong value

    This one-day career guidance workshop is designed to help you work out which career is best for you and how to equip yourself with the skills you need to succeed in that career.

    You will leave at the end of the day with a clear idea of the graduate career that’s best for you, and a step-by-step plan to help you get there. This is a highly practical workshop that includes both group and one-to-one support. You will gain a better understanding of your strengths and weaknesses and leave feeling much clearer about the next step in your career.

    Testimonial

    “Graduate Coach shared expert insights to provide our students with real advice and a constructive action plan to start achieving their career goals, tailored according to course, year of study and future aspirations.”

    - Grace Thompson, vita Student

    What’s covered on the day?

    This course enables you to study key digital concepts and put them into practice.

    Once you have up-skilled and put your skills into practice by completing various digital tasks, our graduate coaches will host virtual career coaching sessions via Skype.

    No, we have helped several candidates to get a job in digital marketing roles. Some of which knew very little about digital marketing before enrolling.

    For the internship element of the course, you will receive in-depth feedback and mentoring on all of the tasks that you complete.

    During the course, you will also complete industry-recognised certifications, some of them will require you to successfully complete online tests.

    Once you have enrolled on the programme, you will gain access to our online digital marketing course.

    As you work your way through the course materials and complete industry-recognised certifications you will be given real tasks to work on.

    Some examples of tasks you will be set includes SEO Audits, social media posts, PPC builds and copywriting. For each task, you will be given a tutorial beforehand and will be given access to resources/tools to help you to complete the tasks.

    You will then have lots of new experiences and skills to add to your CVs and cover letters. Our career coaches will help you to craft these documents, before teaching you how to navigate the job search and nail your interviews.
